Fitness Analysis

real-time posture correction
exercise feedback

This function helps you track your workout posture in real time, supporting 10 common exercises across 6 major muscle groups. If your movement isn't quite right—like squatting too shallow or your feet being too close—the system instantly gives voice or text feedback to guide you. It ensures your form is correct, your workouts are safer, and your results more effective.
